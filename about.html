LARON'S AUTOMOTIVE

Larson’s Automotive of Maplewood serves car, truck and SUV owners in Maplewood, White Bear, Oakdale, North Saint Paul, and Hugo with a complete line of vehicle maintenance and repair services. With today’s busy motorist in mind, Larson’s Automotive provides same-day service for most automotive maintenance or repair jobs. When service requires additional time, Larson’s provides rental car coordination. Customers enjoy a comfortable waiting area for quick-turn repairs.

With over 25 years of experience, family-owned and operated Larson’s Automotive offers small-town personalized service with downtown efficiency. Larson’s takes customers seriously, working to keep cars, trucks and SUV’s running smoothly, with a complete line of services for every vehicle need. From simple oil changes to complex engine re-builds, Larson’s Automotive stands behind their work, including 12 month or 12,000 mile warranties.

Larson’s Automotive proudly offers the Napa Car Care Program. Larson’s is also a Tire 1 dealer and all of Larson’s Automotive mechanics are ASE certified. Additionally, Larson’s handles aftermarket warranty repairs, and will handle the billing with GM, Western Diversified, and Wynns.

Contact Larson’s Automotive to set up your car care appointment today. They can do it all – for your car, truck or SUV.
